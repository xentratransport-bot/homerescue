Our Mission

House Help Services was founded on a simple idea: NYC homeowners shouldn't have to search through sketchy directories or worry about unlicensed contractors when something goes wrong at home. Whether a pipe bursts at 2 AM or you're ready to finally renovate that kitchen, you deserve a single trusted source for vetted, licensed professionals who show up on time and do the job right.

We built House Help Services to be that source. Our network of licensed emergency responders and home improvement contractors covers every zip code across all five boroughs and Northern New Jersey — available 24/7 for emergencies and by appointment for improvement projects.

Who We Are

House Help Services is a New York City home services company with two divisions: an emergency response division operating around the clock, and a home improvement division connecting homeowners with skilled renovation contractors. Every professional in our network is:

  • Licensed — fully credentialed by New York State and NYC for their trade
  • Insured — carrying general liability and workers' compensation coverage
  • Background checked — verified identity and professional history
  • Rated — reviewed by real clients after every job
