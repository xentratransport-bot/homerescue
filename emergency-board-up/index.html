When You Need Emergency Board-Up in NYC

  • Break-ins and burglaries — After a break-in, your doors and windows must be secured immediately to prevent a second entry. We respond to police-released crime scenes across NYC within 30 minutes.
  • Vandalism and graffiti damage — Smashed windows from vandalism leave your property exposed. We secure it fast while police reports are filed.
  • Storm damage — Wind, hail, and flying debris during NYC storms shatter windows and damage doors. We provide emergency weatherproofing and board-up within minutes.
  • Accidental breakage — Accidents happen — a window cracked by a ball, a door shattered during a move, glass broken by contractors. We fix it fast, day or night.
  • Fire damage — After a fire, windows and doors are often compromised. We secure fire-damaged openings to protect the structure from weather and unauthorized entry.
  • Construction accidents — Glass breakage during renovation or construction requires immediate response to keep the site safe and the building secure.

Insurance Claims for Board-Up in NYC

Emergency board-up costs are typically covered by homeowner's and renter's insurance when damage results from a covered event — break-in, vandalism, fire, or storm. House Help Services technicians document all damage from the moment they arrive, providing the photos and reports your insurance adjuster needs for fast claim approval. We work directly with your carrier to get costs reimbursed quickly.

For commercial properties, business owner's policies and commercial property insurance typically cover emergency board-up under the property damage provision. Contact us and we'll work with your commercial carrier directly.
