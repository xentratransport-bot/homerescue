Frequently Asked Questions

Interior painting in Midtown Manhattan typically runs $3–$6 per square foot for walls and ceilings, depending on prep work required, ceiling height, and number of colors. A standard 1BR apartment typically costs $900–$1,800.

We cover and protect all furniture and floors before any painting begins. For larger items we ask that you relocate valuables, but our crew handles plastic sheeting and drop cloths.

A standard NYC apartment (1–2BR) takes 1–2 days. Larger homes and projects requiring extensive prep (wallpaper removal, skim coating) take longer.

We use Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams as our standard lines. We can match any color and work with paint you supply or source it at trade pricing.
